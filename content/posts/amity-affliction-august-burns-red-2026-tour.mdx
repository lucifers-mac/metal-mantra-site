Metalcore titans **The Amity Affliction** and **August Burns Red** are joining forces for one of 2026’s most anticipated heavy tours — the newly revealed **Spring Horizons Tour**. The massive North American co-headline run kicks off April 10 and features support from **Boundaries**, **Heavensgate**, and select appearances from **Dreamwake**. The package will also hit major festivals, including a confirmed stop at [**Sonic Temple**](https://sonictemplefestival.com/).

Fans can secure **pre-sale tickets and VIP packages here**:
[https://tix.soundrink.com/tours/the-springs-horizon-tour-2026](https://tix.soundrink.com/tours/the-springs-horizon-tour-2026)
## Bands Speak on the Long-Awaited Collaboration

**Joel Birch** of The Amity Affliction shared that the two bands have privately discussed teaming up for years, calling this tour “one of the strongest packages we’ve ever brought to the States.” He added that the band is excited to return to what they consider their “second home,” noting the lineup and venue selection make this run especially meaningful.

**August Burns Red** echoed the excitement, describing Spring Horizons as “the can’t-miss metalcore tour of the spring.” ABR confirmed they will be **premiering brand-new songs** during the tour and delivering their signature technical intensity every night. The band also highlighted how stacked the lineup is, praising Boundaries, Heavensgate, and Dreamwake for rounding out the bill.
