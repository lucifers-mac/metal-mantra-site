After years of fans wishing for it, **official festival camping is finally coming to Aftershock** — and 2026 is the year it lands. Set for **Cal Expo in Sacramento**, the new camping setup delivers the full community experience: **crash with your crew, wake up on site, hit the gates early, re-enter whenever you want, and repeat it all weekend long.** More metal news: [News hub](https://metal-mantra.com/news/).

## **Car + Tent, RV Camping, and Shuttle Options**

When the **Early Bird Presale** opens, fans will be able to lock in:

**Car + Tent camping**

**RV camping**

**Shuttle passes** (for those staying off-site)

Everything rolls out with the presale launch, giving diehards first dibs on the most convenient way to stay inside the heart of the action.

## **Exclusive Perks for Campers**

Aftershock isn’t just adding camping — they’re stacking the experience with **camper-only bonuses**.

Leading the charge is **Rock & Brews**, hosting a **Wednesday Night Camping Pre-Party on September 30, 2026**. Campers with wristbands get access to:

Food + drink discounts

**Free mini golf**

**Free batting cages**

All activities located steps from your campsite on the Cal Expo grounds

And the perks won’t stop after night one — **Rock & Brews will roll out weekend-long food and beverage specials exclusively for campers**, with more details coming soon.

## **A New Era for Aftershock**

With on-site camping, early entry, community hangouts, and nightly perks, **[Aftershock 2026](https://aftershockfestival.com/) is shaping up to be the most immersive version of the festival yet**. Stay tuned — more info is on the way, and this is just the beginning of the next evolution of Sacramento’s biggest heavy-music weekend.
