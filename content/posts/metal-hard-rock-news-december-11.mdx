### Triumph — First Tour in 30 Years / 50th Anniversary Tour

After three decades off the road, **[Triumph](https://www.triumphmusic.com/)** have officially announced their first tour in 30 years — a massive return aligned with their 50th anniversary. Fans of classic hard rock and early metal are already buzzing, as this marks the first real chance in a generation to see the Canadian legends back on stage delivering their signature anthems. The band promises a career-spanning set and a production worthy of a half-century milestone, making this one of the most significant comeback tours in recent rock memory.

### Sebastian Bach — North America Tour 2026 + New Video

**Sebastian Bach** has revealed his 2026 North American tour, with **Stitched Up Heart** joining as support. Bach is positioning 2026 as a major year, pairing the tour announcement with the release of a new video for “What Do I Got To Lose?” — a throwback to his classic, high-energy vocal style. The video drop has reinvigorated fans who have waited for a strong new solo moment from the former Skid Row frontman, and the tour is shaping up to be a big one.

###  Brass Against — 2026 U.S. Tour Announcement

**Brass Against** have confirmed an early-2026 U.S. tour with several East Coast dates kicking off January 23. Known for their explosive, horn-driven covers and politically charged performances, the group continues to attract both diehard rock fans and newcomers who appreciate their fusion of metal, rock, hip-hop, and brass arrangements. Their return to U.S. stages signals a busy year ahead.

###  As I Lay Dying — 20th Anniversary “Shadows Are Security” Russian Tour

**As I Lay Dying** are taking *Shadows Are Security* back on the road with a special 20th anniversary Russian tour. The album remains one of the most influential metalcore records of the 2000s, and the band is honoring its legacy by performing in territories that have been eager for a return. For longtime fans, this is a rare opportunity to hear a defining album celebrated front-to-back during a milestone year.
