Fourteen years in, and Danny Wimmer Presents just made the biggest Aftershock ever. The 2026 edition brings **140+ artists across five stages** — including a newly added fifth — to Discovery Park in Sacramento from **October 1–4**. The headliners are My Chemical Romance, Limp Bizkit, Pierce The Veil, and Tool. But the real story this year is everything underneath that top line.

## The Headliners

**My Chemical Romance** closes Thursday night, and they're not just playing a set — they're celebrating **20 years of *The Black Parade***. One of the most culturally significant rock albums of the 2000s, performed by the band that made it, at a festival that will be full of people who grew up with it. That's the booking of the weekend.

**Limp Bizkit** headlines Friday. You know what you're getting. Fred Durst and company are a live draw regardless of what year it is, and Friday's lineup around them runs heavy enough to make the full day worth it.

**Pierce The Veil** takes Saturday in their Aftershock headlining debut. Vic Fuentes and company have earned it — the *Jaws of Life* cycle built them back up to a level where this makes complete sense.

**Tool** closes Sunday. Still the only band where headlining a festival feels like an understatement. No anniversary needed. No special billing. Just Tool.

## The Reunions and Anniversaries

This is where the 2026 bill gets interesting. Danny Wimmer loaded this lineup with moments:

- **Circa Survive** — West Coast reunion show. No performances since 2021.
- **Cavalera** — Exclusive West Coast performance of *Roots* in full. First time the album has been performed live in nearly a decade.
- **Thursday** — Celebrating three milestones simultaneously: 25 years of *Full Collapse*, 20 years of *A City By the Light Divided*, 15 years of *No Devolución*.
- **Wu-Tang Clan** — Rock & Roll Hall of Fame nominees, playing as part of their *Wu-Tang Forever: The Final Chamber* tour.
- **Wolfmother** — 20th Anniversary Tour.
- **Drowning Pool** — *Sinner* 25th Anniversary performance.
- **Municipal Waste** — 25th Anniversary set.
- **From First To Last** — Reunion and rare performance.
- **Kylesa** — Reunion show and first West Coast performance since 2015.
- **Oleander** — Hometown reunion, no shows since 2018.
- **Drop Dead, Gorgeous** — Reunion show.
- **Ünloco** — Reunion show.
- **Red** — Celebrating 20 years of *End of Silence*.
- **The Union Underground** — First California show in 24 years.
- **Chad Gray** — "30 Years of Madness" solo set pulling from both Mudvayne and Hellyeah catalogs.

That's a lot of one-time-only or rarely-seen performances stacked into a single weekend.

## New This Year

2026 marks the first time Aftershock offers **official RV and Car + Tent camping at Cal Expo** — with shuttle access, re-entry included, and a Wednesday night pre-party hosted by Rock & Brews featuring complimentary drinks, mini golf, and batting cages.

A fifth stage — **The Point** — has been added to the expanded festival site, along with a new Cantina del Diablo and Overtime Sports Bar. The **Coast to Coast Pass** bundles Aftershock with Welcome to Rockville in Daytona Beach (May 7–10, 2026).
