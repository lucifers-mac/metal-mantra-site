### 📀 **Rotten Sound** Unleash New Album *Mass Extinction*

Finnish grindcore veterans **Rotten Sound** have released their latest album, *Mass Extinction*, delivering another uncompromising blast of extreme metal aggression. The record reinforces the band’s reputation as one of grindcore’s most relentless forces and arrives amid a strong wave of underground metal releases.

### 🎤 **Evanescence** Bring Jacoby Shaddix Onstage

**Evanescence** surprised fans by welcoming **Jacoby Shaddix** of **Papa Roach** onstage for a live performance of “Bring Me To Life” at KROQ’s Almost Acoustic Christmas. The collaboration delivered a nostalgic yet powerful moment underscoring the song’s lasting impact on modern hard rock and alternative metal.

### 📅 **Infected Rain** Announce Spring 2026 U.S. Tour

**Infected Rain** have announced a spring 2026 U.S. tour, continuing their steady rise within the global metal scene. The Moldovan metalcore outfit’s expanding North American presence reflects growing demand for their emotionally charged and technically aggressive sound.

### 🛠️ **Caliban** Release Deluxe Edition of *Back From Hell*

German metalcore mainstays **Caliban** have released a deluxe edition of *Back From Hell*, featuring unexpected cover tracks from **Tears For Fears** and **Suicide Silence**. The expanded release highlights the band’s versatility while offering longtime fans new material alongside the original record.

### 🔥 Milwaukee MetalFest 2026 Adds Major Acts

Milwaukee MetalFest has announced new additions to its 2026 lineup, including **Pig Destroyer** and **Weekend Nachos**, fueling early anticipation for next year’s event. The expanding lineup continues to position the festival as a key destination for extreme and underground metal.

Power metal veterans **HAMMERFALL** have unveiled their *Freedom World Crusade 2026* North American tour, joined by **Evergrey** and **Elvenking**. The announcement brings major momentum for fans of melodic and traditional heavy metal.

### 🚩 **Kreator** & **Carcass** Confirm 2026 U.S. Tour

Thrash metal titans **Kreator** have confirmed a May 2026 U.S. tour with death metal pioneers **Carcass**. The pairing promises one of next year’s most aggressive tour packages, uniting two influential forces in extreme metal.
