## Q1: January – March 2026

### Confirmed Releases

**Rob Zombie — *The Great Satan* (Nuclear Blast) — February 27, 2026**
Zombie returns with his most ambitious concept record yet, doubling down on the cinematic horror-metal aesthetic he’s refined across decades. Pre-order: [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Rob+Zombie+The+Great+Satan&tag=metalmantra-20)

**Lamb of God — *Into Oblivion* (Epic) — March 13, 2026**
Richmond’s groove-metal kingpins drop their follow-up to *Omens*, and early signals suggest a leaner, meaner record built to destroy festival stages. Pre-order: [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Lamb+of+God+Into+Oblivion&tag=metalmantra-20)

**Exodus — *Goliath* (Napalm Records) — March 20, 2026**
The Bay Area thrash legends return swinging, and if the title is any indication, they’re not coming to play nice. Pre-order: [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Exodus+Goliath+album&tag=metalmantra-20)

**Gaerea — *Loss* (Century Media) — March 20, 2026**
Portugal’s atmospheric black metal force continues their relentless ascent with what promises to be their most emotionally devastating record to date. Pre-order: [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Gaerea+Loss+album&tag=metalmantra-20)

**Black Label Society — *Engines of Demolition* (MNRK Heavy) — March 27, 2026**
Zakk Wylde’s road warrior outfit delivers another slab of bluesy, crushing heavy metal — the kind of record that sounds best cranked through a half-stack. Pre-order: [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Black+Label+Society+Engines+of+Demolition&tag=metalmantra-20)

**Hellripper — *Coronach* (Century Media) — March 27, 2026**
Scotland’s one-man blackened speed metal machine James McBain steps up to a major label with what could be his defining statement. Pre-order: [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Hellripper+Coronach&tag=metalmantra-20)

### Anticipated in Q1

**Poison The Well — *Peace In Place* (SharpTone) — March 20, 2026**
A full decade after going quiet, one of post-hardcore’s most influential bands returns. This one is going to mean a lot to a lot of people. Pre-order: [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Poison+The+Well+Peace+In+Place&tag=metalmantra-20)

## Q2: April – June 2026

### Confirmed Releases

**Corrosion of Conformity — *Good God/Baad Man* (Nuclear Blast) — April 3, 2026**

**Nervosa — *Slave Machine* (Napalm Records) — April 3, 2026**
Brazil’s all-female thrash force keeps the momentum going with their most aggressive and politically charged work yet. Pre-order: [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Nervosa+Slave+Machine&tag=metalmantra-20)

**Immolation — *Descent* (Nuclear Blast) — April 10, 2026**
New York death metal institution Immolation delivers their Nuclear Blast debut, pushing their trademark dissonance and darkness into uncharted territory. Pre-order: [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Immolation+Descent+album&tag=metalmantra-20)

**The Melvins + Napalm Death — *Savage Imperial Death March* (Ipecac) — April 10, 2026**
Two of extreme music’s most iconic acts collaborate for what sounds like a total wrecking ball of a record — the crossover collision you didn’t know you needed. Pre-order: [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Melvins+Napalm+Death+Savage+Imperial+Death+March&tag=metalmantra-20)

**Metal Church — *Dead to Rights* (Rat Pak) — April 10, 2026**
Continuing their second wind, Metal Church press forward with a classic heavy metal effort that honors their legacy without retreading it. Pre-order: [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Metal+Church+Dead+to+Rights&tag=metalmantra-20)

The mythical Slipknot side project that existed only in legend finally sees official release — vinyl only, Record Store Day exclusive. It won’t carry the Slipknot name, but the DNA is unmistakable. A must-grab for collectors and fans alike. Pre-order: [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Look+Outside+Your+Window+album+2026&tag=metalmantra-20)
